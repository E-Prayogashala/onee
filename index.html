ABOUT INSTITUTION

In 2008, Sri M. Anantha Ramudu brought his visionary idea to life by establishing Anantha Lakshmi Institute of Technology and Sciences (ALITS) under the patronage of the Anantha Lakshmi Educational Society. The goal is to create a center for technical innovation and academic excellence in a region that had long needed access to quality higher education. The founding ethos was built on the belief that even in traditionally underserved areas, world-class technical education could be a catalyst for regional growth and social transformation.

INSTITUTE VISION:

To produce globally competent professionals who can contribute to industry, Research & Society at large with environment and ethical consciousness.

INSTITUTE MISSION:

  • M1: Adopt continuous improvements in innovative teaching learning methods and state-of-the-art infrastructure to transform students into industry ready professionals.
  • M2: Bridge the gap between curriculum and industry requirements through preparation for industry readiness and students employability enhancement programs.
  • M3: Integrate ethical, societal, and environmental awareness into the students through curricular and co-curricular activities.

DEPARTMENT VISION:

To produce professional engineers in Electronics and Communication with ethical consciousness to meet local and global standards.

DEPARTMENT MISSION:

  • M1: Providing an ambience of teaching-learning process through the state-of-art infrastructure and modern tools for imparting latest technical skills among the students.
  • M2: Providing student-centric facilities and training for the students to acquire a strong collaboration with Electronics Industry.
  • M3: Providing value-based education to find solutions for societal needs.

PEO’S (PROGRAM EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES)

  • PEO 1: Graduates can acquire competent knowledge in Electronics and Communication Engineering.
  • PEO 2: Graduates can have successful entrepreneurial and professional careers in the core and allied areas of electronics and communication engineering.
  • PEO 3: Graduates can possess communication skills and work efficiently on team-based projects in electronics, and communication with a sense of social responsibility.
