About the Ashram

Sri Shivabalayogi Maharaj Ashramam is a vibrant spiritual center dedicated to the teachings, meditation, and blessings of Swamiji. This sacred space invites all devotees for serene meditation, soulful bhajans, and participation in devotional festivals like Dussara. In our ashramam every devotee can do pooja by themselvs,In our temple we hava BALAYOGI MAHARAJ GURUJI,LOARD SIVA,MATHA PARVATI,SRI VALLI DEVASENA SAMETHA SRI SUBRAHMANESWARA SWAMI temples too.And every year we celebrate swamiji JAYANTHI,TAPASIDDI...functions too.Everyone must visite our temple once u will observe the changes in you. Every year we celebrate dussara 15 days including kalyanam of sivaparvathi,valli deveasena sametha sri subrahamneswara and sridevi bhudevi sametha sri venkateswara swami. this is one of the biggest celebration in every year.
